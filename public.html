Teaching

I lecture at the UC Berkeley School of Information, teaching a series of courses on Applied Machine Learning (W207) as part of the Master of Information and Cybersecurity (MICS) and Master of Information and Data Science (MIDS) graduate programs. In May 2023, I was voted by the graduating Berkeley MICS class for the Distinguished Faculty Award.
