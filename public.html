Public Appearances


Giving conference presentations and trainings enriches my personal research opportunities and helps a nascent community develop an understanding for the subject matter.

  • Founder/co-chair of the AI Village at DEF CON
  • Founder/organizer of the “Data Mining for Cyber Security” meetup group.
    • Started from 0 in May 2015, 2.5k members as of Q2 2018
    • Typically physical attendance ranging from 30 to 300, online attendance (livestream, unique & transient) between 1000 to 3000
    • Speakers hail from large companies like Google/Facebook/LinkedIn/Netflix, as well as startups like Eventbrite, Area1 Security, Sift Science, Graphistry, DataVisor etc.
